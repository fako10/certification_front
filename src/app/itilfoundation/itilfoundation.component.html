ITIL Foundation Practice Tests

Contents

The ITIL Foundation package contains 5 complete tests (totalling to 200 questions).

Taking The Practice Tests


Just like the real exam, you must complete a test in 60 minutes. You may choose to have an untimed test. You can also choose to review the questions one by one, untimed, and answers shown.

Also, just like the real exam, the passing score is 65% (that means, you must have answered correctly, at least 26 questions).

Each complete test has:

  • 1- 150 questions just like the real test
  • 2- full coverage (100%) of the test topics

The look and feel, and features of itestjava.com's ITIL practical test interface is similar to the real test interface at the testing centers.

Quality Of Practice Questions

Our questions are in similar level of difficulty as the real exam questions.
