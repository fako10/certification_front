PSM I - Practice Tests

Contents

PSM I is recognized by the industry as a certification that demonstrates a fundamental level of Scrum mastery

The PSM I practice test package contains 5 complete tests (totalling to 400 questions).

Taking The Practice Tests


Just like the real exam, you must complete a test in 60 minutes. You may choose to have an untimed test. You can also choose to review the questions one by one, untimed, and answers shown.

Each complete test has:

  • 1- 60 questions just like the real test
  • 2- full coverage (100%) of the test topics

The look and feel, and features of itestjava.com's PSM I practical test interface is similar to the real test interface at the testing centers.

Quality Of Practice Questions

Our questions are in similar level of difficulty as the real exam questions.
